FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Find quick answers about tickets, dress code, family accommodations, and more.

What is included with my ticket?
Every ticket includes access to the venue, Iftar dinner, the full evening program, and congregational prayer facilities.
Is the event family-friendly?
Yes. Families and children are welcome, and we aim to provide a comfortable space for guests of all ages.
What should I wear?
We recommend modest, semi-formal or smart casual attire that is comfortable for prayer and photos.
Will there be a prayer space?
Yes, there will be a dedicated prayer area with rugs and space for both brothers and sisters.
Are there vegetarian or dietary options?
We plan to offer vegetarian options and can accommodate common dietary needs. Please share details during registration.
Can non-Muslim friends attend?
Absolutely. Non-Muslim friends and colleagues are welcome to attend and learn more about Ramadan and the community.
Still have questions? Contact the organizing team.
