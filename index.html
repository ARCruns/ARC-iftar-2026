About

An evening to break fast together

ARC Texas Iftar 2026 brings together members from Arab Run Club chapters in Houston, Austin, and San Antonio, along with friends of the community, for one unforgettable Ramadan night.

Break fast at long communal tables, enjoy inspiring speakers and live entertainment, explore vendor booths, enter raffles, and connect with people who care about community, wellness, and giving back.

Whether you’re a student, young professional, creative, founder, or simply looking to experience Ramadan in a welcoming space, this night is designed to help you connect, reflect, and build lasting relationships, while supporting a great cause.

  • • Share Iftar, pray together, and experience Ramadan in community.
  • • Network with ARC members and professionals from across Texas.
  • • Enjoy food, gifts, raffles, vendors, photo moments, and entertainment.
  • • Support meaningful charitable initiatives.

Who you'll meet

  • • Young professionals, founders, creatives, community leaders.
  • • University students and recent graduates looking to grow their network.
  • • Nonprofits, sponsors, and community organizations doing impactful work.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Find quick answers about tickets, dress code, family accommodations, and more.

Do I need to be Muslim/ Arab to attend?
Absolutely not! This event is open to everyone, regardless of background or faith. We welcome anyone interested in experiencing Iftar and connecting with our community.
What is included with my ticket?
Every ticket includes access to the venue, Iftar dinner, the full evening program, activities, and congregational prayer facilities.
Can I bring children?
To maintain the evening’s format and atmosphere, this event is for guests 18+ only.
What should I wear?
We recommend modest, comfortable attire suitable for Ramadan gatherings. Smart casual or traditional Khaliji styles, such as thobes, abayas, or elegant festive wear, keeping the spirit of the occasion in mind.
Will there be a prayer space?
Yes! There will be a dedicated prayer area for both brothers and sisters. Feel free to bring a prayer rug.
What if I have dietary restrictions?
Please contact us in advance. We’ll do our best to accommodate.
Is parking available?
Yes, free on-site parking and street parking will be available.
Still have questions? Contact the organizing team.
